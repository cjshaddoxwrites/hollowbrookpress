Our Mission

Atmospheric fiction with a pulse.

Hollowbrook Press exists for stories that feel discovered rather than manufactured: books with mystery, atmosphere, emotional stakes, and a sense that something old is waiting just beyond the light.

We are beginning with the works of C.J. Shaddox and building toward a carefully curated catalog of fiction across Southern Gothic, supernatural mystery, paranormal romance, young adult fantasy, conspiracy suspense, and related genres.
