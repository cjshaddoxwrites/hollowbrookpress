C.J. Shaddox

C.J. Shaddox writes atmospheric young adult supernatural fiction filled with buried secrets, dangerous loyalties, and small towns hiding impossible truths.

Inspired by folklore, conspiracy mysteries, and the forests and hills of southeastern Oklahoma, the stories blend emotional character journeys with worlds where the supernatural feels inevitable.

The Promised, The Reckoning, and The Breaking are the complete Guardians of the Veil trilogy, published through Hollowbrook Press.
